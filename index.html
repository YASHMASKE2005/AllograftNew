Why Donate Organs?

Organ donation is a selfless act of giving that can save multiple lives. By donating your organs, you give someone else a second chance at life. Organ donors can save up to 8 lives and improve the quality of life for many others through tissue donation.

Organ Donation Facts

Every 10 minutes, someone is added to the national transplant waiting list.

One donor can save up to 8 lives.

There are more than 100,000 people waiting for a life-saving organ transplant.

Despite advances in medicine, more people die every year waiting for an organ than those who receive them.

Organ Donation Process

Step 1: Donor Registration

Donors can easily register by providing their personal details and medical history.

Step 2: Medical Assessment

A thorough medical assessment is conducted to ensure eligibility for organ donation.

Step 3: Matching Process

Our AI-powered algorithm matches donors with recipients based on medical compatibility.

Step 4: Successful Transplant

Once a match is found, the organ transplant procedure is arranged between the hospital and donor.

How You Can Spread Awareness

Need an Organ for Transplant?

When hospitals receive a patient in need of an organ transplant, time is of the essence. Our platform makes it easier for hospitals to request organs by leveraging our exclusive AI matching feature. Our advanced algorithm matches hospitals with the most suitable donor based on medical compatibility, ensuring the best outcomes for the patient.

Hospitals can raise an organ transplant request, which will be queued for AI matching. If a suitable donor is found, hospitals will be notified immediately to proceed with the transplant process.

Before submitting an organ request, hospitals must ensure they are registered on our platform. If you're already registered, simply proceed to the organ request form. If not, please register your hospital first to access this feature.
